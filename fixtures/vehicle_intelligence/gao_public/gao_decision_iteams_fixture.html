Leidos, Inc.

Matter of: Leidos, Inc.

File: B-422999.1

Date: September 12, 2024

DIGEST

1. Protest challenging the Navy's bridge and transition evaluation is denied where the agency reasonably documented schedule and mission continuity concerns.

2. Protest alleging unequal treatment is dismissed where the allegation relies on speculation rather than proposal record evidence.

DECISION

Leidos, Inc., of Reston, Virginia, protests the issuance of a task order to Amentum Services, Inc., under solicitation No. N66001-24-R-0012, issued by the Department of the Navy for ITEAMS mission support.

We deny the protest.
